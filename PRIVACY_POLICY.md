# Privacy Policy for MineBucks

**Effective Date:** 2025-12-15

## 1. Overview

MineBucks ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when you use our mobile application.

## 2. Information We Collect

**We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal information to our own servers.**

* **API Tokens & Cookies:** Your Modrinth API tokens and CurseForge session cookies are stored securely and locally on your device using Android's encrypted DataStore. They are used solely to fetch your revenue data directly from the respective platforms.
* **No Analytics:** We do not track your usage behavior or navigation within the app.
